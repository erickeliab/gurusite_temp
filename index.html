About Us

Welcome to GURUPLANET

Guru Planet Ltd promotes poultry farming and aims to transition informal farming to formal business. The company has piloted Self Mobilized Groups to provide low-cost business consultancy services and marketing among poultry farmers.

In the first phase, Guru Planet Ltd organized capacity-building activities and worked with partners, including government bodies and academic institutions. The company aims to create profitable businesses among poultry farmers, influenced by the high failure rate of poultry farming businesses in the region.

Poultry Commercial Ads 24/7
Guru Planet Ltd is a company that is dedicated to promoting sustainable growth in the poultry industry while also contributing to the development of the Tanzanian economy. The company's main objective is to encourage cooperation between poultry farmers and end users by promoting the production of organic poultry products to various stakeholders. To achieve this goal, Guru Planet works closely with poultry farmers in Tanzania to help them adopt sustainable and organic farming practices. The company provides farmers with training, technical assistance, and access to high-quality inputs such as feed, vaccines, and equipment. By doing so, Guru Planet helps farmers improve the quality and quantity of their poultry products while also reducing their impact on the environment. Overall, Guru Planet's efforts to promote sustainable and organic poultry production are not only beneficial to the environment but also to the Tanzanian economy as a whole. By promoting business cooperation between farmers and end users, the company is helping to create a more sustainable and equitable poultry industry that benefits everyone involved.
